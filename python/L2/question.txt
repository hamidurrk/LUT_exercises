Make a Python program that asks for three numbers and calculates their average. The numbers 
which are inputted can be integers or floats. After this, the program prints 
1. the sum of the given numbers. 
2. the average of the given numbers rounded to 3 decimal places. 
3. the average of the given numbers rounded to the closest integer. 
4. the average of the given numbers as an integer without the decimal part (hint: use int 
function).
